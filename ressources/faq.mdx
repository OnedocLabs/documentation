<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Onedoc ?">
    OneDoc is an API solution that allows developers to create, manage, and
    automate PDF document generation using familiar technologies like React. It
    simplifies the process of document creation, analysis and tracking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is OneDoc suitable for developers with no prior experience in React?">
    Of course. While familiarity with React is beneficial, OneDoc is designed to
    be user-friendly. Moreover, OneDoc offers [SDKs](./sdks) covering many
    programming languages and is constantly evolving to provide the best user
    experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How secure is OneDoc when handling sensitive documents?">
    OneDoc prioritizes security, employing advanced encryption for data in
    transit and at rest, along with stringent authentication protocols to ensure
    that sensitive documents are protected at all times. [More about
    security](./security).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can OneDoc be integrated with other software or platforms?">
    Yes, OneDoc is built for compatibility and can be seamlessly integrated with
    various platforms and systems, enhancing its functionality and user
    convenience. More about [integrations](./integrations)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there an open-source component to OneDoc?">
    OneDoc embraces the open-source community by making portions of its code
    available. This allows developers to contribute, review, and enhance the
    software, fostering innovation and reliability. Check out
    [github](https://github.com/OnedocLabs/onedoc)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What kind of support does OneDoc offer for troubleshooting and queries?">
    OneDoc provides robust support through email support, online call for
    entreprise, and a community forum where users can find assistance for
    troubleshooting and general queries. [Join us on
    Discord!](https://discord.com/invite/uRJE6e2rgr)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there any templates available, or do users need to design PDFs from scratch?">
    OneDoc offers a range of pre-designed templates to get users started.
    However, for specific needs, users can also create custom templates from
    scratch, providing greater flexibility and control. We are currently
    establishing a template marketplace where the community can share their
    creations with others. [More about our templates](/templates/intro)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does OneDoc handle different document formats and layouts?">
    OneDoc is versatile in handling various document formats and layouts,
    allowing users to create a wide range of document types, from simple text
    documents to complex reports with diverse layouts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What are the pricing plans for OneDoc?">
    OneDoc offers various pricing plans, including a free tier with basic
    features, premium plans for advanced capabilities, and a custom entreprise
    plan. These plans are structured to cater to different usage levels and
    business needs. [More about pricing](https://www.onedoclabs.com/pricing/)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What measures does OneDoc take to ensure data privacy compliance?">
    OneDoc is committed to achieve compliance with global data privacy
    regulations such as GDPR and SOC2 Type 2, implementing policies and
    practices that meet these stringent standards. [More about our security
    practices](./security)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
